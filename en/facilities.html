Café

The Florida Café is very spacious with tables and a counter to place orders. It offers a wide variety of food and drinks to meet your needs. It's an ideal place to enjoy a break and relax with friends or alone while having something to drink.

Library

The Florida Library offers soundproof booths for students and professionals seeking a quiet and distraction-free work environment. These booths are designed to provide a private and comfortable space for study and concentration, allowing users to focus on their tasks without being disturbed by external noise.

Florida Pavilion

The Florida Pavilion is a sports complex specializing in indoor soccer and physical training. With a spacious and well-equipped space, it allows players to practice and compete in indoor soccer games in a safe and suitable environment. It is an ideal place for those who enjoy sports and want to improve their skills and physical health.

The Park

The park is a green space located next to Florida. It offers trails, picnic areas, children's playgrounds, sports fields, and beautiful gardens. It is a perfect place to enjoy outdoor activities and relax in nature.
